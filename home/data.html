Hello! My name is Cruz, and I’m a graduate student in math at the University of Oregon. I’m particularly drawn to the beautiful visualizations that math can often create, and I’ve built a number of polished, high-performance web apps for exploring them. I’ve also created interactive notes for many of the classes I’ve taught in pursuit of quality teaching, and occasionally I’ve found time to practice creative writing. All of that is collected here, and no matter what you’re looking for, I think you’ll find something you enjoy.
