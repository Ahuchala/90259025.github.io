Curved Light

Raymarching is an extraordinarily versatile method for rendering 3D scenes of all sorts, from fractals to curved spaces, but this time, things are a little different. By exchanging the straight line that line normally moves along for a different curve, we can produce all sorts of bizarre renderings that showcase the curvature of space, even if it’s artificially imposed.
